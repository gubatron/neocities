Commissions

You're in the right place! Occasionally open commissions depending on time and energy. More information is listed below. Thank you for considering commissioning me!

What I Offer

Digital character art, icons and profile pictures (head/bust) in a cartoon or semi realistic style. I will do full color unless specified otherwise. I also offer landscape art in my style. Turnaround time varies depending on workload and complexity. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

What I Do Not Offer

NSFW art

Overly detailed or hyperrealistic art (extra charge)

Work involving real people without their permission

Offensive, hateful, or discriminatory content

Anything intended for harassment

Excessive gore beyond mild stylization

NFT or blockchain related use

AI training or dataset use

Subjects outside my skill range (I will let you know if something falls here)

Commission Process

Send me an email at smallcarnivorousmammal@gmail.com with a clear description of what you want, reference images, any specifics that cannot be forgotten, and a deadline if you have one

Payment is made up front or split (50% deposit, 50% on completion, PayPal only).
I send a rough sketch for approval. → Revisions are made at the sketch stage. → Once approved, I continue to final rendering. Lastly, the final piece is delivered as a high-resolution file via email or download link.

No refunds after the sketch is approved, unless I cannot complete the commission. One major revision is allowed at the sketch stage. Minor adjustments are allowed during rendering. Major changes requested after approval may incur an additional fee.
